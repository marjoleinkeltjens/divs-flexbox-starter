Hi, we are DynamiQ Voices

We are a young LGBTQIA+ choir based in Utrecht. We were founded in December 2021 and consist of 34 members.

Why do we exist? We started this choir to create a safer space for people from the queer community and to share the joy of singing together.

What's in our repertoire? We mainly sing pop songs. We have songs from Dua Lipa, Billie Eilish and Froukje in our repertoire.

What's up next? Our next concert is for friends and family in Utrecht. In a month we will perform in Bologna at the Various Voices festival.
