Who are we? We started this choir to create a safer space for people from the queer community and to share the joy of singing together.

What's in our repertoire? We mainly sing pop songs. We have songs from Dua Lipa, Billie Eilish and Froukje in our repertoire.

What's up next? Our next concert is for friends and family in Utrecht. In a month we will perform in Bologna at Various Voices.
